## 📊 Design Specifications

### Colors (5 Total)
- **Primary:** Indigo (#6366f1)
- **Secondary:** Cyan (#06b6d4)
- **Accent:** Purple (#a855f7) + Pink (#ec4899) + Teal (#0891b2)

### Typography
- **Font:** Geist (system default)
- **Headings:** Bold (700 weight)
- **Sizes:** Responsive with mobile-first approach

### Spacing
- **Bubble Gap:** gap-3 md:gap-4 lg:gap-6
- **Section Padding:** px-4 py-24 md:py-32
- **Container:** max-w-6xl

### Effects
- **Glassmorphism:** blur(10px) saturate(180%)
- **Transitions:** 300ms cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1)
- **Hover Scale:** 1.10x (110%)
- **Shadow:** shadow-2xl on hover
